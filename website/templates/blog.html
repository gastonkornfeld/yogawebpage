KORNFELD NATALIA YOGA BLOG

The more heart centered teachings of Yoga can help us to release our clinging to these labels and see our inner light. The practice of Yoga is founded in the ancient texts of Patanjali’s Yoga Sutras. These texts are arguably where Yoga, as a philosophy or way of life, began sometime about 2,000 years ago. They describe the Yoga path to enlightenment - a guide to overcoming our “suffering”. I am fascinated at how these ancient texts still hold true today. Although very few modern day yoga practitioners are hoping for enlightenment, there is a something that Yoga can provide. I believe our modern world version on "enlightenment" is joy, happiness and inner peace.
