More about me

Yoga teacher, chef by vocation and eager for birth, I always looked for ways to calm the mental noise and walk a little more calmly. From the beginning I knew that keeping my body active was a good start, but all physical activities always ended up boring me. Until one day suddenly, I came across yoga.

I think the most wonderful thing that I discovered in these years practicing and teaching yoga, is that even when we arrive with the sole objective of improving our physical performance, the road will end up forking, showing us broader knowledge that there can be no balance without imbalance, or physical improvement without better breathing or two practices that are repeated; because our body, like our mind, goes through states that change all day, every day.

Yoga gives us at the end of the road a very simple idea: everything is connected. Our ailments physical, our way of breathing, our daily emotions. And only knowing in depth the way in that the parts operate in the whole we will perhaps be able to feel more a little happier.

Kornfeld natalia
