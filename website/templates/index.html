Each one is a complete and different experience from the other, which invites you to take the necessary breaks in your day, to help you achieve physical, mental and emotional well-being.

What are you going to achieve with these yoga routines?

Build a "comprehensive YOGA routine" that motivates you to maintain it over time

In each of them you will find: a calendar, a guide and video, audio and pdf material. You have the option of doing each one of them separately or purchasing the 3 #yoguiroutines together, for a complete accompaniment plan
